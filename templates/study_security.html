How study security is calculated

Each study is independently assessed across {{ criteria|length }} risk-of-bias criteria. Each criterion is rated H High Risk (score 1), M Medium Risk (score 2), or L Low Risk (score 3). The total security score is the sum of all criteria ratings, ranging from {{ criteria|length }} (all High risk) to {{ max_possible }} (all Low risk). Higher scores indicate stronger, more methodologically secure studies.
